

# Dr. Swathika Rajendran — Premium Personal Brand Website

## Overview
A single-page, cinematic-scroll luxury website for Dr. Swathika Rajendran, a UK-trained Breast Oncoplastic & Reconstructive Surgeon based in Chennai. The site will feel like a premium private surgical brand — elegant, emotionally reassuring, and clinically elite.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Baby Blush Pink (#F6C1D1), Muted Pastel Lavender (#CBB4F6), Pure White, Rose Gold gradients
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (editorial feel) + clean sans-serif body text
- **Style**: Generous whitespace, soft gradients, glassmorphism accents, minimal shadows, subtle animations throughout

---

## Sections (Single Scroll Experience)

### 1. Hero — "Signature Presence"
- Full-screen soft animated gradient background (pink → lavender)
- Dr. Swathika's professional photo placed in an editorial offset layout
- Subtle floating light particles for depth
- Fade-in entrance animations
- **Headline**: "Advanced Breast Surgery with Precision & Compassion"
- **Subheading**: "UK Trained Oncoplastic Surgeon | 600+ Procedures | Reconstructing Confidence"
- Primary CTA button: "Schedule Private Consultation"
- Floating WhatsApp & Call icons with glow hover effects

### 2. About the Surgeon — "Expertise & Philosophy"
- Two-column layout: editorial portrait (left) + glassmorphism bio card (right)
- Animated number counters (6+ years experience, 600+ procedures)
- Credentials: MBBS, MS General Surgery, MCh (UK), GMC Registered, ABS Member
- Full list of surgical expertise areas
- Work history: Kauvery Hospital (current), NHS UK Fellowship, Pearl Health
- Education: Edge Hill University (UK), Sri Ramachandra Institute

### 3. "Breast Health in Today's World" — Thought Leadership
- Editorial-style content section with soft parallax background
- Content on breast cancer awareness, early diagnosis importance, emotional/physical recovery, and the role of oncoplastic surgery in restoring confidence
- Blurred pastel overlay for luxury feel

### 4. Services — Signature Treatments
- Premium card grid with hover animations (3D lift, rose-gold underline)
- Services: Wide Local Excision, Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy, Therapeutic Mammoplasty, Mastectomy, Implant-based Reconstruction, Lipofilling, Chest Wall Perforator Flaps, Breast Reduction & Augmentation
- **Consultation popup**: Elegant modal triggered once per visit when scrolling to this section
  - Fields: Name, Phone, Email, Preferred Date, Message
  - Smooth submission success animation (frontend only — no backend)

### 5. Patient Testimonials — Trust & Transformation
- Luxury carousel slider with soft scaling animations
- Animated 5-star ratings
- Rose-gold styled quote marks
- Placeholder testimonials (to be replaced with real ones later)

### 6. Footer — Signature Close
- Minimal white background with fade-in animation
- Chennai location, contact details, LinkedIn link
- Quick navigation links
- Medical disclaimer & copyright
- Publications mentions

---

## Advanced UX Features
- **Sticky navbar**: Transparent initially, becomes white/blurred on scroll
- **Scroll progress indicator**: Subtle line at top of page
- **Smooth section transitions**: Intersection Observer-based fade/slide animations
- **SEO optimized**: Meta tags targeting "Best Breast Surgeon in Chennai", "UK Trained Oncoplastic Surgeon India", "Breast Reconstruction Specialist Chennai"
- **Schema markup**: MedicalBusiness structured data
- **Accessibility**: Proper heading hierarchy, alt texts, keyboard navigation
- **Performance**: Lazy-loaded images, optimized animations

---

## What's NOT Included (No Backend Needed)
- The consultation form will show a success message but won't send data to a server (can be added later with Lovable Cloud if needed)
- Testimonials will use placeholder content
- No patient portal or login system
